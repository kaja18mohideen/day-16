Page 1: Dog Facts

  • Dogs are descendants of wolves and were domesticated around 20,000 to 40,000 years ago.
  • The Basenji dog is known as the "barkless dog" because it doesn't bark like other breeds.
  • A Greyhound is one of the fastest dog breeds and can reach speeds of up to 45 miles per hour.

Page 2: Cat Facts

  • Cats have five toes on their front paws but only four on their back paws.
  • The oldest evidence of domesticated cats dates back to ancient Egypt around 4,000 years ago.
  • Cats have a unique grooming pattern, and they spend about 30-50% of their day cleaning themselves.

Page 3: Zoo Animal Facts

  • The giraffe is the world's tallest mammal, with a neck that can reach up to 6 feet in length.
  • Elephants are highly intelligent animals and are known for their excellent memory.
  • The poison dart frog is one of the most poisonous animals on Earth.
